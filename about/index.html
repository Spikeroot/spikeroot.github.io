Vint Squeezel: Correspondent of the Citizenry

Citizen Squeezel is the preeminent London authority on the newly-burgeoning field of pseudoanthropology. The peculariarities of the Neath have forced us to reconsider the hard boundaries of anthropology when considering the diversity of apparently sentient beings. The quarterly Journal of Pseudoanthropology documents my time at the frontier of these studies.

Please direct any further inquiries to my office.
